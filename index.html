About Me

I'm Jason, a web developer with 3 years of experience creating and maintaining a wide variety of websites.

Previously I have worked in schools as a Design Technology and CS teacher. I have also worked as a 3D Artist in the computer games industry.

I'm passionate about web development and primarily focus on front-end dev, graphics and UI/UX.

I have a BA(hons) in Graphic Design, A Masters in Computer Animation and a PGCE in Design Technology.

If you like what you see then please get in touch.

Why choose me

I would describe myself as keen, enthusiasic and hard working.

I'm a good communicator and I am friendly and reliable. I love to learn and try hard to stay up to date with the latest web technologies.

I'm good at fitting into teams and am keen to share my experience, skills and expertise.

I love a good problem and the warm fuzzy feeling of satifaction that comes when a solution is found.

My Vision

I love making things that are useful to people, things that look good and work well. Things that are easy to use and make lives better.

I believe in doing things properly and not by halves: my work sticks to standards; code is well written and modular; artwork is sharp and meaningful; user interfaces are logical and intuitive.

Things have to work well, they have to be efficent, they have to be good value.
